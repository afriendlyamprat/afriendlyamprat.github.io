Enemy / Vegetation Article Template Generator

From Pikmin Fanon

This Pikmin Fanon template generator creates the structure of a fanon enemy / vegetation article. The parameters of the generator used apply the guidelines for enemy / vegetation articles that are described in PikminFanon:Enemy and plant article guidelines. For more information on these guidelines, please read the aforementioned Pikmin Fanon article.

Be aware of the fact that this template generator does not support all features that you may want to include in a page. It is designed only as a starting point, and you will likely want to make changes and additions to your page before publishing it.
